



Because many have desired to heare some of the Na­
tives Language, I have here inserted a small Nomen­
clator, with the Names of their chiefe Kings, Rivers, Moneths,
and dayes, whereby such as have in-sight into the Tongues,
may know to what Language it is most inclining; and such as
desire it as an unknowne Language onely, may reape
delight, if they can get no profit.
