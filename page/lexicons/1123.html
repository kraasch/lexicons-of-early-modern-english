Book title
The Young Clerks Tutor; Being A most useful Collection of the best Presidents of Recognizances, Obligations, Conditions, Acquittances, Bills of Sale, Warrants of Attorney, &c. As Also All the Names of Men and Women in Latin;with the day of the Date, the several Sums of Money, and the Addition of the serveral Trades or Employments in their proper Cases, as they stand in the Obligations. Together with Directions of Writs of Habeas Corpus, Writs of Error, &c. to the interior Courts in Cities & Towns; the whose Work newly corrected and augmented; Likewise, The best Presidents of all manner of Concords of Fines, and Directions how to sue out a Fine; with many judicious Observations therein. With many other things very necessary, and readily fitting every mans occasion; As by an exact Table, of what is contained in this Book, more plainly will appear. In this last Impression is also added, several of the best Copies of Court and Chancery Hands now extant
