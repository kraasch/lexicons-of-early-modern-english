THESAVRVS LINGVAE Romanae et Britannicae, tam accurate congestus, vt nihil pene in eo desyderari possit, quod vel Latinè complectatur amplissimus Stephani Thesaurus, vel Anglice, toties aucta Eliotae Bibliotheca: opera et industria Thomae Cooperi Magdalenensis. Summary
Two dictionaries: both Latin-English, the first being a general vocabulary, the second being of proper and place names. A re-edition of a book published in 1565 and indebted to the first Renaissance Latin-English dictionary, written by Sir Thomas Elyot, and published in 1538.
