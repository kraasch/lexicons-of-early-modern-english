Summary
Three books, of grasses (106), edible plants (307), and trees and shrubs (166). (a) "A Table of all such English names, as are attributed vnto the Herbes, Shrubs, and Trees, mentioned in this Historie"; (b) A Supplement or Appendix vnto the Generall Table, and to the Table of English names: gathered out of ancient written and printed Copies, and from the mouthes of plaine and simple Countrie people". Gerard completed Dr Priest's translation of R. Dodoens' Pemptades (1583)
