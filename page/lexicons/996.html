Summary
APpellativa common names arborum of trees erunt shall be muliebria feminine, ut as alnus an alder tree, cupressus a cypresse tree, cedrus a cedar tree, spinus a sloe tree mas is masculine, oleaster a wild Oliue tree, mas is masculine, & and siler an osier, suber cork, thus frankincense, robur an oake, que and acer a maple, sunt are neutra neuters. (a4v)
