Summary
(a) A Latin grammar with English word-citations, "Dictionariolvm vocum Anglicarum, cuæ passim in libello occurrunt"; (b) a bilingual "Analysis Grammatica, ad nostra huius artis præcepta unitè conformata", English-Latin glossary, English-Latin word-entries for English function words (indexed to the Latin grammar); and (c) an English-Latin Chaucer vocabulary (the last of which appears only in the British Library copy), "Vocabulva Chauceriana quædam selectiora, et Minvs Vvlgaria Ipsæ Hodie Poetarum deliciæ, vnà cum eorum significatis"
