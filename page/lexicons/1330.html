sample: BITTER WINE. Take Gentian root a dram and a half; Centaury, Camomile flowers, tops of Carduus, Roman woodworm of each three drams; Rhubarb one dram, yellow peel of orange half a pound; Cloves, Mace and Nutmegs, of each one dram; infuse all these two days and nights, and then strain it; drink a glass fasting and another before dinner or supper. If you think proper you may add two ounces of filings of steel.
