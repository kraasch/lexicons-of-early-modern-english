Summary
12 words of Wexford dialect and equivalents in Early Modern English; this part, in The Historie of Irelande from the first habitation thereof, vnto the yeare 1509. Collected by Raphaell Holinshed, and continued till the yeare 1547. by Richarde Stanyhurst, has separate title page, foliation, and register (STC) Book III, Ca. 5, “Of prouision made for the poore,” sigs. d2v-d3r, cites Thomas Harman and 23 names for men and women idle vagabonds.
