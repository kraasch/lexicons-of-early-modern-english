explanations: English
<i>Scala</i> in Lattine signifieth a Ladder, which name is giuen as wel for the likenes as the vse: For the likenes because it hath lines like steps in a Ladder, and spaces, in which the <i>Cleues</i> or Keyes do stand, for the vse because it serueth the learner to climbe vp to the knowledge of Musicke: or that he must ascend and descend therewith, as men doe in building.
