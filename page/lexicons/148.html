Summary
Two tables of simples describe how to use them and what effects they have: (a) alphabetical table of simples, rarely in two names each (e.g., Alecost, Costmary, or Maudlinwort" (¶3r)); and (b) alphabetical table of the effects or symptoms or diseases treated by these simples (e.g., "¶ Abhorring meat", "¶ Abortion", etc. (a1r)). They bracket a 702-page collection of articles on the simples.
