Summary
(a) Latin-English dictionary of simples (2v-33v); (b) English-Latin dictionary of simples (35r-57r); (c) Latin lists of simples classified by their properties (59r-). Owned by John Lumley, baron Lumley (1534?-1609); and attributed to "Doctor Halle," presumably Dr. John Hall (1574/5?–1635), but the othe earlier John Hall may be intended (see LEME 103 [1565])
