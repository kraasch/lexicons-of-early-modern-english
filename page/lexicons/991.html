sample: Demissos animo, ac tacitos vitare memento.
Qua flumen placidum est, forsan latet altius vnda.

Memento] Be myndfull
Vitare] cauere, to eschewe,
[Sub homines] demissos animo] natura tristes, men whiche are naturally sad:
Ac] pro(&)
Tacitos] semper tacentes, & consilium dissimulantes, men that be silent: that is to saie, whiche speake not a worde, and alwaie hide their secretes and counsels.
Vnda] aqua, The water
Latet] lieth hid
Forsan] fortasse, perchaunce
Altius] profundius, more deepe (sub, tibi) there
Qua] pro vbi, where
Flumen est placidum] fluuius est tranquillius, the Riuer is quiet, that is to saie, where it runneth softly. (H5v-6r)
